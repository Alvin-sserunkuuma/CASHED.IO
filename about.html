About Us

Cashed is a web-based application focusing on supporting individuals with funds and financial advice to fight poverty in Uganda. This application is supported by the Government of Uganda, under the Ministry of Finance and Planning.

Our Mission

To empower individuals to break the cycle of poverty through sustainable programs that provide access to economic opportunities and social services, fostering resilience, self-sufficiency, and long-term well-being.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has the opportunity to achieve their full potential, free from the constraints of poverty, and thrive through equitable access to resources, opportunities, and support systems.

Objectives

  • To promote economic opportunities
  • To improve people's welfare
  • To monitor and evaluate progress

Cashed is a newly created application designed in 2024, initiated by the Government of Uganda, developed by BIST DAY A GROUP 10.
