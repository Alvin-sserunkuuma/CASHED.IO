Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your return policy?
A: There is no return amount to funds issued to beneficiaries, however maximum and appropriate utilization of funds is expected from the members.
Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team at cashedapp256@gmail.com or contact us on the numbers on the contact page.
Q: Do you offer international support?
A: No, we only offer our services to citizens of Uganda with a valid national ID card.
Q: I don't know how to use the website?
A: We can help you out at our offices in Makerere, Kampala or our field agents can help reach you at the contacts provided in the contact page.
Q: I failed to register or login/I haven't got a notification/I haven't received the funds?
A: Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
Refresh the page.
Reboot your device.
Endeavour to use simple and easy to recall usernames and passwords.
Ensure you enter correct details.
Contact us for further help.
Q: Do you have headquarters around the country?
A: Unfortunately we don't have branches countrywide but we have mobile field agents around the country.
Q: Can one be a member of this program?
A: With time, the founders would consider giving room for more members to join.
Q: What is the purpose of this program?
A: This program aims at rendering help to the youth to enable them bring out their potentials in economic development.
